The Technique

Each accent uses 4 layers: background gradient (dark → color-tinted → dark), inner glow (inset box-shadow), outer glow (ambient light spill), and text glow (text-shadow). On hover, all intensities increase + a shimmer sweeps across. The result isn't "colored" — it's illuminated.
