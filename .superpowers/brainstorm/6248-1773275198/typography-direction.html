A: Classic Serif

Georgia / Crimson Text — old-world book feel. Serifs evoke manuscripts, fantasy novels, and aged tomes. Body text stays highly readable.

B: Hybrid — Display Sans + Body Serif

Bold condensed sans for headings (impact, authority), serif for body text (readability, warmth). Think pulp fiction poster meets old book. Best of both worlds.

C: Modern Sans (Keep Current Direction)

Stay with clean sans-serif but use heavier weights and tighter spacing for headings. The grimoire feel comes from color/texture, not the font itself. Simplest change.

Context

This choice cascades into everything — cards, modals, buttons, navigation. The font sets the baseline tone for "dark fantasy retro pulp fiction." We'll pick specific Google Fonts after settling on direction.
