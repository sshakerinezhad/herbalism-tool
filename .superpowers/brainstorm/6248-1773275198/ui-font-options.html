Cinzel

Roman imperial capitals. Authoritative, structured, made for small labels. All-caps by nature — great for buttons and stat badges. Creates a "carved inscription" vibe for UI chrome. Dark Souls energy.

Marcellus

Roman inscriptional — similar vibe to Cinzel but softer, more hand-chiseled. Slightly less commanding, slightly more approachable. Only regular weight (no bold), so relies on letter-spacing and caps for emphasis.

Spectral

Sharp, precise serif. Wide weight range (200-800). Designed for digital screens — crisp at all sizes. Slightly more modern than Cinzel/Marcellus but still old-world. Best readability of the three.

Cinzel (buttons/labels) + Mono (stats only)

Hybrid: Cinzel for buttons, labels, and nav. Monospace for numeric stats (INT, DC, quantities). The mono gives stats a "game HUD" feel — mechanical precision amid the fantasy aesthetic. 4-font stack but each has a clear role.
